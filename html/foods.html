FOOD
DELICACIES

The Albay province is known for its savory and extremely spicy cuisine, which will surely captivate your tongue as you reach for a glass of cold water.

Pili Nuts

Albay is known for its homegrown premium pili nuts. The usual favorites are crispy candies pili nuts with honey, roasted and salted pili, marzipan, butternuts, caramelitos, pastillas, and sesame seed coated pili nuts.

Pinangat

Pinangat is a version of laing, a famous native chili-hot dish of a little town of Camalig, Albay which is a blend of ground pork, coconut milk, diced shrimps, and chili wrapped in gabi leaves. This dish is well-known among Bicolanos because of its distinctive taste that a Bicolano could perfect.
