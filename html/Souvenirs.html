Abaca

Musa textilis or Abaca is a burlap-styled fabric manufactured from durable banana tree fibers processed from Abaca plants. When it comes to this material, the Bicol region in the Philippines is where it is produced in massive numbers. Some products that could be produced from Abaca are furniture, carpets, rugs, bags, and many more.

Souvenirs

Like other tourist places, the province of Albay also has its features. Their souvenirs range in different sizes. However, they are all similar in that they symbolize something in Albay.

Putsan Ceramics

For over a millennium, Tiwi, a municipality in Albay, has been noted for its handmade clay ceramics. Seventy percent of Tiwi's residents make a living selling clay pottery in the local market.
